# Privacy Policy for Clarity for Facebook

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Overview

Clarity for Facebook is a browser extension that helps users customize their Facebook browsing experience by hiding unwanted content such as Reels, sponsored posts, and suggestions.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Data Storage

- All user preferences and settings are stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's storage API
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No user behavior is monitored or recorded

## Permissions Used

- **Storage**: Used to save your content filtering preferences locally on your device
- **Host Permissions (facebook.com)**: Required to modify Facebook pages according to your preferences

## Third-Party Access

- We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties
- We do not use any third-party analytics services
- We do not display advertisements

## Data Security

- All data remains on your local device
- You can clear extension data at any time by removing the extension or clearing browser data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
